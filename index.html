About me

I have learned a lot about team play while working as a head server and an assistant manager in a busy restaurant for the past 10 years and also learned a lot about how to communicate with the team and customers.

I started studying web development by myself at the end of last year, and I got more interested, so I finished a 3-month boot camp program at Learning Fuze to improve my coding skills.

While taking to this program, I studied and worked more than 60 hours a week (more than 800 hours in total) in the same environment as working in the field. I also learned how to finish projects and tasks in a timely manner and how to communicate with other developers.

It was tough to take this program, but it was a very precious experience for me and it was a good enough time to learn many things and improve my coding knowledge.

Currently, I am working on several portfolios and am constantly learning new and reviewing lacking knowledge.
