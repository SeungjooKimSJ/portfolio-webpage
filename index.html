About Me

Team play and communication with others are one of the most important skills in society.
Fortunately, the two biggest skills I learned while working as an assistant manager and head server at busy restaurants over the past 10 years were team play and communication.
I have also intern for a CPA office during my school years and it has taught me how to meet deadlines by daily, weekly, and monthly.

However, I had a desire to pursue more intersting, exciting, and challenging in the field that I have never walked on.
This was becoming a web developer and signed up for class at the LearningFuze in Irvine, California.

While taking to this program, I studied and worked more than 60 hours a week (more than 800 hours in total) in the same environment as working in the field.
Through this time, I realized how to finish projects and tasks within the due date and how important it is.
Also learned how to communicate with other developers.

It was tough to take this program but was a very precious experience for me.
This is because it was enough time to learn a lot and improve my coding knowledge.

Looking back my 10 years, I was a team player as well as a very detail oriented individual who can meet the deadline is very important and this is my biggest asset.
Currently, I am working on several portfolios to get a job, and constantly learning with passion.
